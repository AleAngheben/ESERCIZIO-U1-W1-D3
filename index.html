Fociaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorit starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce



Ingedients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all–purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1–2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with paring knnife if desire, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium–sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to acctually knead the dough, though I sometimes it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
